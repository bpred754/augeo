Augeo is a tool that gives you the power to view your interests in a quantitative way. It's common sense... the more you do something the better you become. In this case, we categorize your online activity into different skills and award experience depending on the activity type. As your experience grows, you will gain levels and be ranked among other Augeo users. We have a large vision for Augeo and our intention is to expand to a vast set of interfaces, from other sites and blogs to IOT devices. The possibilities are endless. In a nutshell, Augeo is the gamification of life.
